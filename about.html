About GeoL

GeoL — Laboratory for Deep Mining Geoscience & Engineering — is a research group focused on the geomechanical and geotechnical challenges of deep underground mining. Our work combines rock mass characterization, numerical modeling, and applied technological innovation, developed alongside academic institutions and mining industry partners.
