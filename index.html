Research areas
Where we work

Three areas of study that sustain our applied research in deep mining geomechanics.

01

Geotechnical characterization

Geotechnical units, structures, and rock mass quality in underground mines such as El Teniente and Chuquicamata.

02

Deep underground mining

In-situ stress challenges, excavation stability, and geomechanical design at great depth.

03

Innovation and electrification

Mining technology, process electrification, and renewable energy in underground operations.
